About Me

I am a pre-doctoral Research Fellow at TCS Research, where I’m co-advised by Dr. Lovekesh Vig and Dr. Gautam Shroff. My work broadly focuses on “Program Synthesis from Natural Language”.

I completed my bachelor’s degree in Electrical & Electronics engineering from BITS Pilani (Goa Campus). Previosuly, I had the opportunity of working at MIDAS-IIITD, Cognizer Inc, and Cognitive Neuroscience Lab at BITS Goa during my undergraduate studies. I also play around with competitive Data Science and am currently a Competitions Expert on Kaggle.
